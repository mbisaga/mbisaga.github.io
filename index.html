An Augmented Reality iOS App Written in Swift

SummitARis an augmented reality iPhone app that labels landscapes around you using a 360° camera view. This app is the perfect travel and navigation companion to hikers, climbers, search and rescue workers, military personnel,surveyors, and world travelers. SummitARuses your GPS and compass to figure out where you are, even as you move around. As you pan with the live view, labels will pop up over landmarksto orient you. Each label expands on touch to reveal detailed information about its location. There is also a handy map view to find places around you from a bird’s eye perspective. The app also allows crowdsourcing of data, which means you can add a locationto share your favorite spot with the world.
